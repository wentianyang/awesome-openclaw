# Contributing to Awesome OpenClaw

[English](./CONTRIBUTING.md) | [简体中文](./CONTRIBUTING.zh.md)

Thank you for considering contributing to the OpenClaw ecosystem! We welcome submissions of new tools, plugins, skills, or showcases.

---

## Submission Guidelines

- **Relevance**: Resources must be directly related to OpenClaw.
- **Quality**: Links must be public and stable.
- **Accuracy**: Descriptions should be concise and accurate.

## How to Contribute

1. **Fork** this repository.
2. Add your resource to the relevant section in `README.md` (English) or `README.zh.md` (Chinese).
   - Format: `- [Name](URL) - Short description.`
3. (Optional) Update `resources.json` to keep structured data in sync.
4. Submit a **Pull Request**.

## Compliance & Opt-out

- **Opt-out**: As a non-profit public index, we respect authors' choices. If you want your project removed or modified, please open an Issue or email [legal@getclawai.com](mailto:legal@getclawai.com). We will respond within 24 hours.
- **Disclaimer**: Resources are curated from the community. Use them at your own risk. OpenClaw team is not responsible for the stability or safety of third-party tools.
